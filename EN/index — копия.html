    about us

    An experience marked by a range of achievements will of the individual and residential housing renovation and expansion of public buildings, be it restaurants, shops and schools

    HEAD OF TECHNOLOGY

    Volia Zarichnii

    Currently studying architecture at the Ecole Nationale Supérieure d'Architecture de la Villette, he has a real gift to give life to ideas from a blank page. His creativity and talent in 3D imaging allows our prospects and customers to visually realize the final rendering of their project, right from its launch.

    FOUNDER

    Alina Payet

    Background: After graduating from ENSAV in Versailles University in 2006, she spent nearly seven years working for different architectural agencies in Paris area before going on her own in 2011.
    Style: Keeping one foot in the past and the other in the future. She manages to create imaginative pieces with a timeless quality.
    Whatever the project, her goal is crystal clear: "to find a balance between the elegance of the structure and the pleasure of the project in use".

    LEAD DESIGNER

    David Zarichnii

    Designer and interior architect,David has defined his own language using natural shapes which are supple, organic, fluid and structured at the same time. A double passion for the material and for the shape embodied by a double faceted course of studies: first of all following a course in Sculpture on Metal (at the Ecole Nationale Supérieure des Arts Appliqués et des Métiers d'Art), then design at the Arts Décoratifs in Strasbourg
