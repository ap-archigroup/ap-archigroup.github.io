    about us

    An experience marked by a range of achievements, from private residences and housing developments, to renovations and extension of public buildings, including restaurants, shops and schools

    FOUNDER

    Alina Payet

    After graduating from ENSAV at Versailles University in 2006, she spent seven years working for different architectural agencies in the Paris area before setting up her own practice in 2011.
    Her style: Keeping one foot in the past and the other in the future. She manages to create imaginative pieces with a timeless quality.
    Whatever the project, her goal is crystal clear: "to find a balance between the elegance of the structure and the of living within it".
