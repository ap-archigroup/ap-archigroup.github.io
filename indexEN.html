FOUNDER

Alina Payet

After graduating from ENSAV at Versailles in 2006, she began her professional career in several Parisian agencies before starting her solo career in 2011.

Style: One foot in the past, the other in the future, she designs timeless projects that prioritize quality of life.

Her goal: "To find a balance between the elegance of the structure and the pleasure of use. One of the keys to success is knowing how to work with space according to the lifestyles and wishes of the people who will live there".
